Questions

Everything you're wondering, answered.

I've literally never trained before. Is this really for me?

Honestly, that's most people who walk in. Good Juju is built for beginners. Your coach starts with the basics and moves at your pace, and there's no such thing here as being "too unfit to start."

What actually happens at the free assessment?

A friendly chat about your goals, then a simple movement screen so we can see how you move. You'll leave knowing exactly how we'd help. It's free, and there's no pressure to sign up for anything.

I have an old injury or some aches. Can I still train?

Usually, yes. Your coach builds your training around your body and starts gently, and we've got physiotherapists in the same building if you'd like their input. We work with you, never push you into pain.

Will I be training in front of a big crowd?

Nope. It's just you and your coach, in a calm space that never feels like a packed commercial gym. No queues for machines, no one watching, no judgement.
